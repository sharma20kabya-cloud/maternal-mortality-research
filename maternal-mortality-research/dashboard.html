🔬 Key Determinants of Maternal Mortality

−0.87
Pearson r — Institutional Delivery vs MMR
States with higher institutional delivery rates show dramatically lower MMR. Kerala (99.8% delivery) → MMR 19 vs Assam (84.6%) → MMR 195.
−0.79
Pearson r — Female Literacy vs MMR
Every 10% rise in female literacy correlates with ~15 point drop in MMR. Education empowers women to seek timely antenatal care.
−0.72
Pearson r — ANC4 Visits vs MMR
4+ antenatal care visits detect complications early. States with high ANC4 coverage consistently report lower maternal death rates.

🔍 Key Research Findings

📍 Assam, UP & MP consistently record India's highest MMR — all above 130.
🏥 States with >80% institutional deliveries show 3× lower MMR than states below 50%.
📚 Kerala — highest literacy (96.1%) — has India's lowest MMR at just 19.
🌍 Sub-Saharan Africa accounts for ~70% of all global maternal deaths.
🎯 India needs to reduce MMR by 27 more points to meet the SDG 3.1 target of 70.
